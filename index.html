Luncher is a tool connecting schools in need of funding to donors across the country. Every child should have enough to eat.

Our Vision

Luncher connects schools in need of funding for their school lunch programs with donors across the country. When you become a part of Luncher you join a movement of people who believe that no child should go hungry.

What Can Your Money Do?

Did you know that just $1 can provide up to 10 meals for hungry kids? Through our partnerships we are able to make your dollars go further when finding solutions to child hunger. What could be a better investment?

Summer Meals

For the 5 million kids in the United States who rely on free or reduced price lunch, or who need it, but aren't getting it, summer time can be a struggle. When school is not in session, these kids rely on free summer meal programs run by schools and community centers. Using Luncher you can help fund these meals, so that kids don't go hungry no matter what time of year it is.

The Breakfast Gap

Children across the United States come to school hungry everyday because there isn't enough to eat at home. This negatively affects their academic achievement, health, and happiness. Right now there is a huge gap between the number of kids who aren't recieving adequate nutrition, and the number of kids who are recieving free breakfasts at school. Recent estimates suggest approximately 3 million children who need breakfast at school aren't getting one. Luncher is working hard to help schools close that gap, but we need your help.
